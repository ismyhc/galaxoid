    Controls

    Dodge the enemies by using the arrow keys. You can also shoot the enemies by pressing space bar for score bonuses! Don't miss the falling life and score powerups for even more bonuses!

    Don't forget to add your name to the Online Highscores!

    Known bugs in Alpha v0.1

    • Player receives random score and life bonuses without actually hitting a score or life bonus
